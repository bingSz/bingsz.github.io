  在陈伯孝的《现代雷达系统分析与设计》中给出了模糊函数的若干性质，但书上缺少了详细证明，此处给出这些性质的详细证明。

0. 模糊函数的频域形式#

$$ \chi(\tau, f_d\ ) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f-f_d\ )U^*(f)e^{-j2\pi f \tau} \mathrm{d}f $$

证明：

  记$u(t)$的傅里叶变换为$U(f)$，则$u^*(t)e^{-j2\pi f_dt}$的傅里叶变换为$U^*(-f-f_d)$，$u^*(t+\tau)$的傅里叶变换为$U^*(-f)e^{j2\pi f\tau}$

  根据帕塞瓦尔定理可得

$$ \begin{align*} \chi(\tau, f_d)=&\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d t} \mathrm{d}t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(-f-f_d)U^*(-f)e^{j2\pi f\tau} \mathrm{d}f \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f-f_d)U^*(f)e^{-j2\pi f \tau} \mathrm{d}f \end{align*} $$

1. 关于原点的对称性#

$$ |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )| = |\chi(-\tau, -f_d\ )| $$

证明：

  由定义得

$$ \begin{align*} \chi(-\tau, -f_d\ ) =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t-\tau)e^{-j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d} t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t+\tau)u^*(t)e^{-j2\pi f_d\ (t+\tau)} \mathrm{d} t \text{(令}t-\tau \rightarrow t\text{)} \\ =& e^{-j2\pi f_d\ \tau}[\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d} t]^* \\ =& e^{-j2\pi f_d\ \tau}\chi^*(\tau, f_d\ ) \end{align*}$$

  因此

$$ |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )| = |\chi(-\tau, -f_d\ )| $$

2. 原点的最大值性#

$$ |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )| \leq |\chi(0, 0)| $$

证明：

  由定义式可知

$$ \begin{align*} |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )| =& |\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d} t| \\ \leq &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|u(t)u^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t}| \mathrm{d} t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|u(t)u^*(t+\tau)| \mathrm{d} t \tag{2.1} \end{align*}$$

  对$|\chi(0, 0)|$，有

$$ |\chi(0, 0)| = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t) \mathrm{d} t = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|u(t)u^*(t)| \mathrm{d} t \tag{2.2} $$

  比较$(2.1)$与$(2.2)$可知

$$ |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )| \leq |\chi(0, 0)| $$

3. 模糊体积不变性#

$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 \mathrm{d} \tau \mathrm{d} f_d\ = |\chi(0, 0)|^2 $$

证明：

  已知

$$ \begin{align*} &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 \mathrm{d} \tau \mathrm{d} f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t_1} \mathrm{d} t_1 \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u^*(t_2)u(t_2+\tau)e^{-j2\pi f_d\ t_2} \mathrm{d} t_2 \mathrm{d} \tau \mathrm{d} f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1+\tau)u^*(t_2)u(t_2+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ (t_1-t_2)} \mathrm{d}t_1\mathrm{d}t_2\mathrm{d}\tau\mathrm{d}f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1+\tau)u^*(t_2)u(t_2+\tau)\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}e^{j2\pi f_d\ (t_1-t_2)} \mathrm{d}f_d\mathrm{d}t_1\mathrm{d}t_2\mathrm{d}\tau\ \tag{3.1} \end{align*} $$

  考虑常函数$y(\omega)=1$的逆傅里叶变换，我们有

$$ \frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}e^{j\omega t} \mathrm{d}\omega=\delta(t) $$

  因此可得

$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}e^{j2\pi f t} \mathrm{d}f = \delta(t) $$

  于是$(3.1)$可变为

$$ \begin{align*} &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 \mathrm{d} \tau \mathrm{d} f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1+\tau)u^*(t_2)u(t_2+\tau)\delta(t_1-t_2)\mathrm{d}t_1\mathrm{d}t_2\mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+\tau)u^*(t)u(t+\tau)\mathrm{d}t\mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t+\tau)u^*(t+\tau)\mathrm{d}\tau u(t)u^*(t)\mathrm{d}t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t+\tau)u^*(t+\tau)\mathrm{d}(t+\tau) u(t)u^*(t)\mathrm{d}t \\ =& [\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t)\mathrm{d}t]^2 = |\chi(0, 0)|^2 \end{align*} $$

4. 自变换特性#

$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 e^{j2\pi (f_d\ x - \tau y)} \mathrm{d} \tau \mathrm{d} f_d\ = |\chi(x, y)|^2 $$

证明：

  前半过程与性质3证明类似，可得

$$ \begin{align*} &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 \mathrm{d} \tau \mathrm{d} f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1+\tau)u^*(t_2)u(t_2+\tau)e^{-j2\pi \tau y}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}e^{j2\pi f_d\ (t_1-t_2+x)} \mathrm{d}f_d\mathrm{d}t_1\mathrm{d}t_2\mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1+\tau)u^*(t_2)u(t_2+\tau)e^{-j2\pi \tau y}\delta(t_1-t_2+x)\mathrm{d}t_1\mathrm{d}t_2\mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+\tau)u^*(t+x)u(t+x+\tau)e^{-j2\pi \tau y}\mathrm{d}t\mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}[\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t+\tau)u^*(t+x+\tau)e^{j2\pi (t+\tau)y} \mathrm{d}(t+\tau)]^*u(t)u^*(t+x)e^{j2\pi ty}\mathrm{d}t \\ =& \chi(x, y)^*\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+x)e^{j2\pi ty}\mathrm{d}t \\ =& \chi(x, y)\chi(x, y)^* = |\chi(x, y)|^2 \end{align*} $$

5. 模糊体积分布限制#

$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 \mathrm{d}\tau = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\chi(\tau, 0)|^2 e^{-j2\pi\tau f_d\ } \mathrm{d}\tau $$

$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 \mathrm{d}f_d\ = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\chi(0, f_d\ )|^2 e^{j2\pi\tau f_d\ } \mathrm{d}f_d\ $$

证明：

  先证明第一个式子，根据性质0可知等式左边为

$$ \begin{align*} &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 \mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f_1-f_d\ )U^*(f_1)e^{-j2\pi f_1 \tau} \mathrm{d}f_1 \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U^*(f_2-f_d\ )U(f_2)e^{j2\pi f_2 \tau} \mathrm{d}f_2 \mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f_1-f_d\ )U^*(f_1)U^*(f_2-f_d\ )U(f_2)\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}e^{-j2\pi \tau (f_1 - f_2)} \mathrm{d}\tau\mathrm{d}f_1 \mathrm{d}f_2 \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f_1-f_d\ )U^*(f_1)U^*(f_2-f_d\ )U(f_2)\delta(f_1-f_2)\mathrm{d}f_1 \mathrm{d}f_2 \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f-f_d\ )U^*(f)U^*(f-f_d\ )U(f) \mathrm{d}f \end{align*} $$

  而等式右边为

$$ \begin{align*} &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\chi(\tau, 0)|^2 e^{-j2\pi \tau f_d\ } \mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f_1)U^*(f_1)e^{-j2\pi f_1 \tau} \mathrm{d}f_1 \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f_2)U^*(f_2)e^{j2\pi f_2 \tau} \mathrm{d}f_2 e^{-j2\pi \tau f_d\ } \mathrm{d}\tau \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f_1)U^*(f_1)U(f_2)U^*(f_2)\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}e^{-j2\pi \tau (f_1 - f_2 + f_d)}\mathrm{d}\tau \mathrm{d}f_1 \mathrm{d}f_2 \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f_1)U^*(f_1)U(f_2)U^*(f_2)\delta(f_1 - f_2 + f_d) \mathrm{d}f_1 \mathrm{d}f_2 \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}U(f-f_d\ )U^*(f)U^*(f-f_d\ )U(f) \mathrm{d}f \end{align*} $$

  比较可知，第一个式子等式左右两边相等，因此成立。

  对于第二个式子，等式左边有

$$ \begin{align*} &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\chi(\tau, f_d\ )|^2 \mathrm{d}f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t_1} \mathrm{d} t_1 \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u^*(t_2)u(t_2+\tau)e^{-j2\pi f_d\ t_2} \mathrm{d} t_2 \mathrm{d}f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1+\tau) u^*(t_2)u(t_2+\tau)\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}e^{j2\pi f_d\ (t_1 - t_2)} \mathrm{d}f_d\ \mathrm{d} t_1 \mathrm{d} t_2 \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+\tau)u^*(t)u(t+\tau)\mathrm{d}t \end{align*} $$

  等式右边有

$$ \begin{align*} &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\chi(0, f_d\ )|^2 e^{j2\pi\tau f_d\ } \mathrm{d}f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t_1} \mathrm{d} t_1 \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_2)u^*(t_2)e^{-j2\pi f_d\ t_2} \mathrm{d} t_2 e^{j2\pi \tau f_d\ } \mathrm{d}f_d\ \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1) u(t_2)u^*(t_2) \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{j2\pi (t_1 - t_2 + \tau) f_d\ } \mathrm{d}f_d\ \mathrm{d} t_1 \mathrm{d} t_2 \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t_1)u^*(t_1) u(t_2)u^*(t_2) \delta(t_1 - t_2 + \tau) \mathrm{d} t_1 \mathrm{d} t_2 \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t)u^*(t+\tau)u^*(t)u(t+\tau)\mathrm{d}t \end{align*} $$

  比较可知，第二个式子等式左右两边相等，同样成立。

6. 组合性质#

若$c(t)=a(t)+b(t)$，则有

$$ \chi_c(\tau, f_d\ ) = \chi_a(\tau, f_d\ ) + \chi_b(\tau, f_d\ ) + \chi_{ab}(\tau, f_d\ ) + e^{-j2\pi f_d\ \tau}\chi_{ab}^*(-\tau, -f_d\ ) $$

其中，$\chi_{ab}(\tau, f_d\ )$为互模糊函数，如下定义

$$ \chi_{ab}(\tau, f_d\ ) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}a(t)b^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t $$

证明：

  将$c(t)$代入模糊函数定义式中，可得

$$ \begin{align*} \chi_c(\tau, f_d\ ) =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}(a(t)+b(t))(a^*(t+\tau)+b^*(t+\tau))e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}a(t)a^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t \\ &+\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}b(t)b^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t \\ &+ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}a(t)b^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t \\ &+ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}b(t)a^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t \\ =& \chi_a(\tau, f_d\ ) + \chi_b(\tau, f_d\ ) + \chi_{ab}(\tau, f_d\ ) + \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}b(t)a^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t \tag{6.1} \end{align*} $$

  注意到

$$ \begin{align*} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}b(t)a^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d}t =& [\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}a(t+\tau)b^*(t)e^{-j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d} t]^* \\ =& [\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}a(t)b^*(t-\tau)e^{-j2\pi f_d\ t} \mathrm{d} t]^* e^{-j2\pi f_d\ \tau} \text{(令} t \rightarrow t-\tau \text{)} \\ =& \chi_{ab}^*(-\tau, -f_d\ )e^{-j2\pi f_d\ \tau} \tag{6.2} \end{align*} $$

  将$(6.2)$代入$(6.1)$中，可得

$$ \chi_c(\tau, f_d\ ) = \chi_a(\tau, f_d\ ) + \chi_b(\tau, f_d\ ) + \chi_{ab}(\tau, f_d\ ) + e^{-j2\pi f_d\ \tau}\chi_{ab}^*(-\tau, -f_d\ ) $$

7. 时间和频率偏移的影响#

若$v(t)=u(t-t_0)e^{j2\pi f_0\ (t-t_0)}$，则

$$ \chi_v(\tau, f_d\ )=e^{j2\pi (f_d\ t_0 - f_0\ \tau)}\chi_u(\tau, f_d\ ) $$

证明：

  将$v(t)$代入原公式可得：

$$ \begin{align*} \chi_v(\tau, f_d) =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}v(t)v^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d t} \mathrm{d} t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}u(t-t_0)e^{j2\pi f_0(t-t_0)}u^*(t-t_0+\tau)e^{-j2\pi f_0(t-t_0+\tau)}e^{j2\pi f_d t} \mathrm{d} t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} u(t-t_0) u^*(t-t_0+\tau) e^{-j2\pi f_0 \tau}e^{j2\pi f_d t} \mathrm{d} t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} u(t-t_0) u^*(t-t_0+\tau) e^{-j2\pi f_0 \tau}e^{j2\pi f_d (t-t_0)}e^{j2\pi f_d t_0} \mathrm{d} (t-t_0) \\ =& e^{j2\pi (f_d t_0 - f_0 \tau)} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} u(t-t_0) u^*(t-t_0+\tau) e^{j2\pi f_d (t-t_0)} \mathrm{d} (t-t_0) \\ =& e^{j2\pi (f_d t_0 - f_0 \tau)}\chi_u(\tau, f_d) \end{align*}$$

8. 信号周期重复的影响#

如果单个脉冲信号$u(t)$的模糊函数为$\chi_u(\tau, f_d\ )$，将信号$u(t)$重复$N$个周期得到的信号$v(t)=\sum_{i=0}^{N-1}c_iu(t-iT_r)$，其中$c_i$表示复加权系数，$T_r$为脉冲重复周期，则$v(t)$的模糊函数为

$$ \begin{align*} \chi_v(\tau, f_d\ ) = \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}e^{j2\pi f_d\ mT_r}\chi_u(\tau+mT_r, f_d\ )\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m}c_i^*c_{i+m}e^{j2\pi f_d\ iT_r} \\ + \sum_{m=0}^{N-1}\chi_u(\tau-mT_r, f_d\ )\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m}c_ic_{i+m}^*e^{j2\pi f_d\ iT_r} \end{align*} $$

证明：

  将$v(t)=\sum_{i=0}^{N-1}c_iu(t-iT_r)$代入模糊函数的定义式中，得到：

$$ \begin{align*} \chi_v(\tau, f_d) =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}v(t)v^*(t+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_d t} \mathrm{d}t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} [\sum_{i=0}^{N-1}c_iu(t-iT_r) \sum_{i=0}^{N-1}c_i^*u(t-iT_r+\tau)]e^{j2\pi f_d t} \mathrm{d}t \\ =& \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \sum_{i=0}^{N-1}\sum_{j=0}^{N-1}c_ic_j^*u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-jT_r+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_dt} \mathrm{d}t \tag{8.1} \end{align*}$$

  考虑积分号内的求和，注意到：

$$\begin{align*} &\sum_{i=0}^{N-1}\sum_{j=0}^{N-1}c_ic_j^*u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-jT_r+\tau) \\ =& \sum_{i=0}^{N-1}\sum_{j=0,j<i}^{N-1}c_ic_j^*u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-jT_r+\tau) \\ &+\sum_{i=0}^{N-1}\sum_{j=0,j \geq i}^{N-1}c_ic_j^*u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-jT_r+\tau) \tag{8.2} \end{align*}$$

  将$(8.2)$代入$(8.1)$中可得

$$\begin{align*} \chi_v(\tau, f_d) =&\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \sum_{i=0}^{N-1}\sum_{j=0,j<i}^{N-1}c_ic_j^*u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-jT_r+\tau) \\ &+\sum_{i=0}^{N-1}\sum_{j=0,j \geq i}^{N-1}c_ic_j^*u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-jT_r+\tau) \mathrm{d}t \end{align*}$$

  对于积分号内前一项求和，作增量代换，我们有

$$ \begin{align*} &\sum_{i=0}^{N-1}\sum_{j=0,j<i}^{N-1}c_ic_j^*u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-jT_r+\tau) \\ =& \sum_{i=0}^{N-1}\sum_{m=1}^{i}c_ic^*_{i-m}u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-(i-m)T_r+\tau) \\ =& \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}\sum_{i=m}^{N-1}c_ic^*_{i-m}u(t-iT_r)u^*(t-(i-m)T_r+\tau) \\ =& \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m}c_{i+m}c^*_iu(t-(i+m)T_r)u^*(t-iT_r+\tau) \end{align*} $$

  根据性质7可得，$u(t-(i+m)T_r)$的自相似度为$e^{j2\pi f_d(i+m)T_r}\chi_u(\tau, f_d)$，因此积分号内前一项求和为

$$\begin{align*} &\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m}c_{i+m}c^*_iu(t-(i+m)T_r)u^*(t-iT_r+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_dt} \mathrm{d}t \\ =& \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m} c_{i+m}c^*_i \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} u(t-(i+m)T_r)u^*(t-iT_r+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_dt} \mathrm{d}t \\ =& \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m} c_{i+m}c^*_i \\ & \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} u(t-(i+m)T_r)u^*(t-(i+m)T_r+mT_r+\tau)e^{j2\pi f_dt} \mathrm{d}t \\ =& \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m}c_{i+m}c^*_ie^{j2\pi f_d(i+m)T_r}\chi_u(\tau+mT_r, f_d) \\ =& \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}e^{j2\pi f_d\ mT_r}\chi_u(\tau+mT_r, f_d\ )\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m}c_i^*c_{i+m}e^{j2\pi f_d\ iT_r} \end{align*}$$

  对于积分号内后一项求和同理，因此综合可得

$$ \begin{align*} \chi_v(\tau, f_d\ ) = \sum_{m=1}^{N-1}e^{j2\pi f_d\ mT_r}\chi_u(\tau+mT_r, f_d\ )\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m}c_i^*c_{i+m}e^{j2\pi f_d\ iT_r} \\ + \sum_{m=0}^{N-1}\chi_u(\tau-mT_r, f_d\ )\sum_{i=0}^{N-1-m}c_ic_{i+m}^*e^{j2\pi f_d\ iT_r} \end{align*} $$
